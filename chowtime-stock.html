After Auditor Visit — Reset Baselines

Charges only apply to new shortage since the last checkpoint — the same standing gap won't be charged twice. Once your branch auditor visits and does an official POS stock adjustment, the old gap is resolved. Tap this to clear the running baseline so future counts start fresh from zero, and nothing old gets carried forward or accidentally credited back.
